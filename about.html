ORIGN STORY


Our community origin story starts with a fateful search for a BLACKPINK WhatsApp group. After joining the largest international group, I met a fellow Sri Lankan Blink and we became fast friends. As we bonded over our shared love for the BLACKPINK, we noticed that there was no official BLACKPINK fan community in Sri Lanka. So, we decided to take matters into our own hands and create one ourselves. We started with an official WhatsApp group, which quickly grew in size and popularity. We expanded our reach through social media and a YouTube channel, and soon we had established ourselves as a force to be reckoned with in the Sri Lankan fan community. With the help of many dedicated fans, we organized donation campaigns and formed partnerships with other organizations. Today, we are proud to have established the first Sri Lankan fanbase website for BLACKPINK fans. Our origin story is one of friendship, passion, and a shared love for BLACKPINK that brought us together and continues to drive us forward.
